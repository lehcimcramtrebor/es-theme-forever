
You can customise your personnal colorset in "my_colorset.xml" file.



Enter your color in hexadecimal (6 characters, example: 28787D for blue/grey  or ffffff for white or 000000 for black).




You can pick color hexadecimal color with thi online tool: https://htmlcolorcodes.com/

Don't enter more than 6 characters, transparency is use in theme's colorsets for gamelists.
