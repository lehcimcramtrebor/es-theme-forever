Here you can put your 16/9 Full Screen Videos.

First in theme configuration/theme style, select "CUSTOM FULL SCREEN VIDEOS".

Put a file named background.mp4, this will display your loop video in full screen behind the cabinet in systemview for all systems.

Put ".mp4" files named by theme (megadrive.mp4, snes.mp4 etc..), this will display video for each system separately..


You can put for example a background.mp4, a megadrive.mp4 and a snes.mp4, this will display background.mp4 for all system
except megadrive and snes.
