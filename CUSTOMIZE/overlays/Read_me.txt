Here you can personnalize one of theme screen overlay by system. For example you can have Spiderman overlay
for just Sega Megadrive.

It's simple,


1 - Choose your screen overlay in "_overlays" folder.

2 - Copy the ".xml" file you want (example: overlay_spiderman.xml), don't cut but copy OK?

3 - Paste your file here ( CUSTOMIZE/overlays/ )

4 - Rename your file with theme name , for example rename "overlay-spiderman.xml" to "megadrive.xml".

Sega megadrive now display Spiderman screen overlay.
