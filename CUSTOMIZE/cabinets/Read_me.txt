Here you can personnalize one of theme cabinet by system. For example you can have Grey cabinet
for just Sega Megadrive.

It's simple,


1 - Choose your cabinet in "_cabinets" folder.

2 - Copy the ".xml" file you want (example: cabinet_grey.xml), don't cut but copy OK?

3 - Paste your file here ( CUSTOMIZE/cabinets/ )

4 - Rename your file with theme name , for example rename "cabinet-grey.xml" to "megadrive.xml".

Sega megadrive now display Grey Cabinet.
